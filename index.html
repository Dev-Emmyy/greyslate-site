Frequently Asked Question

What if I can't move out by the time we close?

If you need more time, we'll do our best to accommodate you. For some of our clients, we're able to advance them the deposit on their next lease or cover their moving expenses.

Can I sell my inherited home if there's a disagreement among heirs?

We do everything possible to facilitate an agreement that makes everyone involved happy, delivering the fairest results given your family history. We'll work to negotiate an agreement between heirs before going to court, but if a court resolution is needed to close the estate then we can assist with legal expenses to accomplish this for you.

What if I don't want my tenants to know I'm selling?

We can do a discreet home assessment that doesn't alert your tenants that you're looking to sell. We can also explore options like a temporary lease-back or moving assistance to help make the transition as smooth as possible.
